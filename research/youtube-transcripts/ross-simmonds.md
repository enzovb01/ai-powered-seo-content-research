# Knowledge Extracted

## Mental Models

- Publishing is the starting point, not the finish line.
- Content value compounds through distribution.
- Distribution should be treated as a system.

## Best Practices

- Repurpose every high-value asset.
- Match content format to each platform.
- Measure distribution performance continuously.

## Actionable Ideas

- Build a content distribution workflow.
- Automate repurposing with AI.
- Reuse evergreen content quarterly.
